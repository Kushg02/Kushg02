    Meet Kush

    The ZUBE Robot is a friendly, companion robot that can be used in all aspects of society. This revolutionary robot can protect your home, tutor your children, and clean your house. ZUBE is an open-source technology platform, allowing developers to study, change, and distribute the technology and apply it to new spheres such as medicine.

    Classroom Assistant

    • Increases Student Engagement
    • Help Teach Language Lessons
    • Telepresence

    According to a recent case study performed in Singaporean elementary schools, the interactive robots “provide visualization of complex ideas, helping children to learn and collaborate better.” At ZUBE Robotics, the intention is not for robots to replace the teacher, but rather assist the teacher and enhance the learning process for students. ZUBE Robots can provide personalized approach to students who have autism or ADHD - a population susceptible to falling behind in the classroom.
